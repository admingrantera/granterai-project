Find the answers to the most common questions about GranterAI below.

How is this different from other grant databases?

The key difference is our AI-powered matching and drafting. Traditional databases rely on you to perform manual keyword searches. Our AI understands the context of your mission and compares it against the actual giving history of thousands of foundations. We show you who is already funding causes like yours and then help you write the first draft of the outreach letter. It's a proactive, not reactive, approach.

What is GranterAI and how does it work?

GranterAI is an intelligent grant-seeking platform. You tell us about your nonprofit's mission and programs in your profile. Our AI analyzes your profile and compares it against millions of public tax records (IRS 990s) to find foundations that have a demonstrated history of funding organizations just like yours. We then use that data to help you instantly draft personalized outreach letters.

Will the AI write my entire grant proposal for me?

No. GranterAI is designed to be a powerful assistant, not a replacement for your expertise. The AI generates a personalized, data-driven first draft of your Letter of Inquiry (LOI). This draft serves as a strong starting point, saving you hours of time and writer's block. We believe the best proposals are a synergy of machine intelligence and your human passion.

What's included in the 14-day free trial?

Your free trial gives you full access to all the features of the plan you select. You can find matches, generate documents, and use the Grant Pipeline to see the full power of GranterAI. No credit card is required to start your trial.
