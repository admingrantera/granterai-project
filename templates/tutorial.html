GranterAI Quickstart Guide

Follow these simple steps to find your first top-tier funder in minutes.

Step 1

Build Your Profile

The first and most important step is to tell our AI about your organization. After you sign up, you'll be taken to the onboarding page. Fill this out with as much detail as possibleâ€”the more specific you are about your mission, programs, and community, the better your matches will be.

Step 2

Discover Your Matches

Once your profile is complete, navigate to your Dashboard. Our AI will analyze your profile and populate it with your first set of foundation matches, each with an "Opportunity Score" to help you prioritize.

Step 3

Manage Your Pipeline

On the dashboard, add promising foundations to your personal grant-tracking board, the Pipeline. Drag and drop cards between columns like "Researching," "In Progress," and "Submitted" to keep your fundraising efforts organized.

Step 4

Generate Your First Draft

Go to the "Document Generator" tool. Select a foundation from your pipeline, then click "Generate." Our AI will instantly write a personalized, data-driven first draft of a Letter of Inquiry, ready for you to edit and send.
