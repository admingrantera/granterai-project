Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you will retain access until the end of your current billing period.

What happens after my 14-day free trial ends?

We will send you a reminder email before your trial ends. You can then choose to subscribe to a paid plan by adding your payment information. If you do nothing, your account access will be paused, but your data will be saved.

Do you offer discounts for very small non-profits?

We strive to make GranterAI accessible to all organizations. Please contact us to discuss your specific situation, and we'll do our best to work with you.
